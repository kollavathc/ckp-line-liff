# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Customers who open the CK Pharmacy catalog inside the LINE app (LIFF) to browse medical equipment. They're on mobile, often mid-conversation with the pharmacy's LINE account, looking for a specific product or category. The job to be done: find a product, check its details, and send an inquiry to the pharmacy's LINE chat with minimal friction.

## Product Purpose

A customer-facing product catalog (browse, search, filter by category, view product detail) that hands off to a real conversation with the pharmacy via LINE chat. Success is a fast, trustworthy browsing experience that ends in a completed inquiry message, not a purchase flow.

## Brand Personality

Trustworthy and calm. Pharmacy-adjacent: reassuring, clean, no-nonsense. Not clinical-cold, not playful/casual.

## Anti-references

No specific anti-references called out. Avoid generic SaaS/marketing flourishes (gradient text, hero-metric templates, decorative glassmorphism) that would undercut the trustworthy, utilitarian feel of a pharmacy tool.

## Design Principles

- Clarity over decoration: the product and the inquiry action are always the focus.
- Calm trust: reassuring, pharmacy-appropriate tone, not clinical or sterile.
- Fast on mobile: this is used inside LINE on a phone, mid-task.
- Preserve brand identity: existing purple (`primary` `#763f9a` / `secondary` `#9f52a4`) carries brand recognition and should stay legible and prominent even as the surface lightens.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No specific WCAG level requested. Maintain standard basics: body text contrast ≥4.5:1, comfortable tap targets for mobile/LINE in-app browser use.
